Workout tracker

This app was created to satisfy people that want to record their workouts and share results with geek-friends. Yes... Only geek, becuse to use this app you need to have GitHub account to log in.

Imagine you are on the gym, and as on standard usual day you want to record your progress. You do it for yourself. But... Your friends sitting on the couch and watching TV. In the same time you add your workout they can see that you work hard looking after your healthy lifestyle. They might think that if others are on gym they should also try to be more active.

Eventually - you will help people with this app!
